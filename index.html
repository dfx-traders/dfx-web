About

DFX Traders is a proprietary trading firm specializing in supplying liquidity to regulated crypto currency exchanges in Europe and United States. By using bespoke pricing algorithms and superior execution, we are able to offer competitive spreads whilst at the same time enforcing strict risk management controls. The firm trades exclusively with its own capital and therefore has no clients other than the exchanges on which it operates.
